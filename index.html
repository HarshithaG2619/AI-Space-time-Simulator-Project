About the Simulator:

This simulation shows how massive objects warp space-time, like a bowling ball on a trampoline. This 'dip' is gravity!

Add planets or black holes to see them deform the fabric.

Black Holes: These are like super, super heavy bowling balls. They make really deep dips!
